Getting Started

Setup Time: 15 minutes

Class Period: 45-50 minutes

Materials Needed:

  • Computers/tablets for student access
  • World map or atlas
  • Student investigation notebooks
  • Detective badges (optional)

Lesson Structure

Introduction (10 min): Mission briefing and case assignment

Investigation (25 min): Student exploration and evidence gathering

Analysis (8 min): Team discussion and findings

Debrief (7 min): Geographic concepts and next steps

Role-Playing Tips

Encourage students to "stay in character" as detectives. Use detective terminology:

  • "Investigate" instead of "study"
  • "Evidence" instead of "information"
  • "Case files" instead of "worksheets"
  • "Mission briefing" instead of "instructions"
